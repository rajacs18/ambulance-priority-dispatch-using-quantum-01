The Problem

Why Quantum for Ambulance Dispatch?

Classical computers struggle with combinatorial explosion. Quantum optimization offers a fundamentally better path.

⚡

Factorial Complexity

For N ambulances, classical brute-force requires N! checks. At 20 ambulances, that's 2.4 quintillion combinations.

🧮

QUBO Formulation

We encode the dispatch problem as a Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization — natively compatible with quantum algorithms.

⚛️

QAOA Algorithm

The Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm uses parameterized quantum circuits to find near-optimal solutions efficiently.

🚑

Real-World Impact

Faster, smarter dispatch assignments based on Distance × Traffic × Severity — saving critical minutes in emergencies.
